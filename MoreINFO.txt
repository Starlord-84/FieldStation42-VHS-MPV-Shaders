FieldStation42 VHS & CRT Shader Collection

A set of custom MPV GLSL shaders designed to emulate authentic analog video.

Features:
• CRT display simulation
• RF chroma interference
• VHS tracking errors
• Horizontal VHS blur
• Broadcast-style analog artifacts

Unlike most VHS filters, these shaders aim to reproduce subtle imperfections found in real consumer VHS tapes and late 80s/early 90s television broadcasts rather than heavily degraded footage.

Originally developed for the FieldStation42 retro television project.
